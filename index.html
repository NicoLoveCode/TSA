About the show

Rupland City is known for its healthy cookies. But Dr. Prime is stealing everyone’s cookies and making them unhealthy. Now it’s up to Sir Denis and Mr. Mike to stop Dr. Prime in his tracks. Will they succeed? Or will Dr. Prime force Rupland City into obesity?




























Characters


Dr.Prime is an evil scientist who is constantly trying to conquer Rupland City by stealing people’s healthy cookies.

Sir Denis - The hero who has dedicated his life to stopping Dr. Prime and his evil-doings.
